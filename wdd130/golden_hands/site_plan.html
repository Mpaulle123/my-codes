Overview

Purpose

Have you ever bought something online while living in Africa? You surely experienced how tricky it is to make a payment and be delivered. My online shopping web site will provide a wide variety of products espacially jewelries and accessories near to you at very competitive prices and different delivery ways. It will allow the buyer to pay by any available and secured way in his country. The web site will be so greatly intuitive that both sellers and buyers will not need instruction or training to use it.

Audience

This site is intended for men and women from heighteen years old and higher living mostly in Africa, especially in countries where it is not easy to make payements online with credit cards. It is also for those who would like to discover and buy products made in Africa by african and from all over the world at good and competitive prices. The website is also intended for both artisanal and industrial producers, resellers, wholesalers and retail sellers.
